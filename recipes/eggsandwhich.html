Egg Sandwhich

Description

This is a simple egg and bagel sandwhich recipe for the purposes of this assignement

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 everything bagel
  • 4 strips of bacon
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat a large frying pan to medium heat.
  2. Add 4 tbs of butter to pan
  3. Once pan is heated add egg to pan and bacon to pan, cook to desired tempature
  4. While the egg and bacon are cooking heat up bagel in toaster
  5. Add slice of cheese ontop of cooked egg
  6. Once bagel is toasted, put your egg with cheese on bottom slice and apply bacon to top of egg. Garnish with desired condiments and enjoy.
